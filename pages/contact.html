Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about our boutique grooming experience.

01 Booking & Scheduling

How do I book an appointment?

The easiest way to book is via our online portal using the “Book Now” button. Alternatively, you can message us on Instagram for specialized requests.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for any cancellations. Cancellations within the 24-hour window may be subject to a rescheduling fee.

02 Services & Policies

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve the entire Niagara Region, including St. Catharines, Niagara Falls, and Welland.

Do you have specific breed policies?

We welcome all breeds. However, for first-time visits of specialized breeds like Poodles or Doodles, we recommend a consultation.

Still have questions?

Our dedicated support team is ready to assist your pet’s needs.

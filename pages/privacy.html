Privacy

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and only collect the information necessary to provide a smooth, professional, and high-quality grooming experience.

Information We Collect

We may collect information when you contact us, request services, or provide details about your pet.

  • Your name and contact details
  • Your pet’s information
  • Messages, consultation notes, or booking details

How We Use Information

Your information is used to communicate with you, manage appointments, maintain service records, and provide grooming services.

We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Platforms

If you contact us through third-party platforms such as Instagram, those services may collect and process information under their own privacy policies.

Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for communication, appointment history, service records, and administrative purposes.

Contact

If you have privacy-related questions or would like to request an update to your information, please contact us at thegroomer.ca@gmail.com.

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect operational or legal changes.

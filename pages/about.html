Founding Story

A Letter from Sunyoung

"When I first held a pair of grooming shears, I didn't see a haircut. I saw a way to communicate care to a being that speaks without words."

Dear Valued Pet Owners,

I hope this message finds you and your beloved pets in great health and happiness. My name is Sunyoung, and I have had the immense privilege of serving as a groomer in the Niagara region for over a decade. I have been blessed with the opportunity to form incredible relationships with so many clients and their wonderful dogs. Most have entrusted me with their pets' care for their entire lives - I am deeply grateful humbled by the certitude placed in me.

I have experienced the joy and fulfillment of meeting a great deal of eager pet owners and working diligently to meet the growing demand. However, after years of operating at such a high pace, I found myself yearning for a steadier, more sustainable rhythm. The following transition allowed me to continue providing the exceptional care and attention your dogs deserve, without compromising on quality.

Throughout this new chapter, I have been exclusively partnering with clients who are particularly dedicated to the consistent upkeep of their dogs' health and well-being. I seek to build long-term, genuine relationships with pet owners who share my high standards of care and are committed to maintaining their pets' hygiene and appearance through regular, pre-booked appointments.

My goal is to reach our highest potential together, providing precisely what is required to keep your pets happy, healthy, and looking their best. I am excited about the prospect of continuing this journey with you and your pets, focusing on quality, consistency, and the genuine connection that has always been at the heart of my practice.

Thank you for your understanding and continued support. I look forward to building meaningful bonds with those who share my vision and dedication to the greatest standards of pet care.

Warm regards, Sunyoung

Care first, cut second

At thegroomer.ca, my services are tailored to discerning pet owners who seek the very best for their beloved companions.

My mission is to elevate the standards of dog grooming by providing personalized, holistic care and artistry that honors the unique needs and personalities of each dog. I strive to build enduring relationships with both pets and their owners so that every visit is a serene, familiar and positive experience. Through expert techniques and anchors in ethics of care, I aim to enhance the well-being and happiness of our furry friends.

As the founder and namesake of thegroomer.ca, I have been perfecting my craft for 12 years as one of the most exclusive dog grooming technicians in the Niagara Region. Certified and specially knowledgeable in canine health and behavior, I have cared for over one thousand clients with unwavering dedication, many for their entire lifetime. My approach is not just about grooming; it's about fostering genuine connections and comprehensive values so your pets may feel their best selves.

Within my passion and pursuits in excellence, I am committed to continuing my legacy of top-tier, personalized care.
